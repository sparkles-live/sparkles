# SPARKLES - Satellite Light Contamination on Space Telescopes

This repository contains the website for the SPARKLES project, dedicated to the Nature article "Estimating artificial light contamination from satellites on space telescopes".

## About

SPARKLES (Satellite Pollution Assessment and Reduction for Key astronomical Light Emission Studies) is a research initiative focused on quantifying and mitigating the impact of artificial satellite constellations on astronomical observations from space-based telescopes.

## Website Sections

- **Summary**: Overview of the Nature article and key findings
- **News**: Recent developments and related articles
- **Satellite Trail Contamination Forecast**: Predictive models for future contamination levels
- **Considerations**: Key factors and mitigation strategies
- **Publications**: Research papers and related literature
- **About SPARKLES**: Project information and team details
